Aster Share

Share a recording, without giving up control.

Aster Share is the sharing side of Aster. Publish a recording, or the page Claude composed from it, as a private, end-to-end encrypted link. Because Claude builds the page, the share fits the content: a walkthrough, a report, a doc with the video embedded, not a one-size video player. Everything else stays on your Mac, and you decide what goes out, who can see it, and for how long.

How it works

From recording to private link

01

Publish from Aster

Pick Publish to Aster Share on any recording or Claude artifact. Nothing is uploaded until you choose to do this.

02

Get a private link

Aster Share returns a private, unguessable link. Send it to anyone. They don't need an account or the app to open it.

03

Stay in control

You get an admin key that lives in your Mac's Keychain. Use it to check views or delete the share whenever you want.

Security first

Sharing that stays on your terms

Aster Share is built so that sharing is a deliberate choice, never the default. Shares are end-to-end encrypted, so whoever hosts them can't read them.

Opt in, every time

Recordings are made and processed on your device. A file only leaves your Mac when you publish it on purpose.

You hold the key

Each share has an admin key stored only in your macOS Keychain. It is never shown on a page or carried in a link.

Links expire on their own

Every share has an expiry date, so links don't quietly stay live forever after you've moved on.

Delete it anytime

Revoke a share with one click and the link stops working right away.

Private viewer links

Viewer links are random and unguessable, and the people you share with never see your admin key.

The same idea, end to end

Aster Share follows the same approach as the rest of Aster. Your data stays local until you act. The recording is captured and processed on your device, and sharing is a separate, deliberate step. When you do share, the link is private, it expires, and you can take it down at any time.
