Why it's different

Not a notetaker. A space you hand off.

Most tools give you a summary to keep. Aster gives your client a living space — everything from the engagement, queryable, with the receipts attached, and safe to share because it's encrypted before it leaves your Mac.

A space per client

Every call, screen-share, document, and decision from an engagement rolls up into one private space — not a pile of recordings, but a single place that represents the relationship.

They ask, it answers with receipts

Your client — or their AI — asks "what did we decide about pricing?" and gets the answer with the exact clip, transcript line, or document attached. No scrubbing through a video.

Safe to share by design

Capture is bot-free and local, and what you share is encrypted on your Mac before it leaves. We store only ciphertext and never hold the key — so putting client material in a space doesn't mean trusting our servers.

How it works

From the call to a space they can use

1

Bring the work in

Import recordings, transcripts, and documents you already have — or capture a call in Aster, locally and bot-free. The space doesn't care where the material came from.

2

It becomes a structured, AI-readable space

On your Mac, Aster turns the raw recording into transcript, on-screen text, clicks, and documents — the source material an AI can actually read and answer from, not just a video file.

3

Hand the client a link

Share the space as an encrypted page. The client opens it, reads the summary, watches the clips, and asks it questions directly — and you control who can open it.

Who this is for

Consultants, agencies, lawyers, fractional execs, onboarding specialists, product teams, founders — anyone who runs engagements and has to hand something back at the end. If a fixed summary isn't enough, this is built for you.
