Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

The short version. Aster is a Mac app that records your screen and keeps everything on your device. Nothing leaves your Mac unless you take an explicit action, like sending a recording to Claude or publishing a share link. We run no accounts, no ads, and no analytics or tracking, and we do not sell or share your data.

Who we are

Aster ("we", "us") is a macOS application and a small set of supporting web services, operated as a personal project. This policy explains what data Aster handles, where it goes, and how long it is kept. It covers the Aster Mac app, the Aster Share service at share.asterlabs.app, the website at asterlabs.dev, and the Aster connector for Claude.

Local-first by design

When you record with Aster, the capture and everything derived from it is written to files on your Mac: the video, audio transcript, detected clicks, on-screen text (OCR), window titles, and the correlated summary. They are not uploaded anywhere by default. Aster has no background sync and no telemetry. A recording stays private to your machine until you choose to do something with it.

When data leaves your Mac

Data only leaves your device when you take one of these actions:

1. Sending a recording to Claude (Anthropic API)

If you generate an artifact or otherwise ask Claude to work with a recording from inside the app, Aster sends the relevant text derived from that recording (and, where applicable, the prompt you chose) to the Anthropic API using your own API key, which you store in your Mac's Keychain. That request is governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy. We do not receive, proxy, or store a copy of those requests. They go directly from your Mac to Anthropic.

2. Publishing with Aster Share

When you publish a recording and/or its artifact as a shareable web page, the content is uploaded to the Aster Share service, which runs on Cloudflare (Workers, R2 object storage, and a D1 database). What gets stored depends on the share type:

  • Encrypted (zero-knowledge) shares: the video and content are encrypted on your Mac before upload. We store only ciphertext. The decryption key is placed in the link's URL fragment (the part after #), which is never sent to our server. It is held by the browser and used to decrypt in-page. We cannot read the contents of an encrypted share.
  • Plain shares: the video (if any) and the artifact HTML are stored as-is so the page can be viewed by anyone with the link. Use an encrypted share if the content is sensitive.

For every share we store minimal metadata needed to serve it: random access keys, a title, optional duration, file size, timestamps, and view/download counts. We do not require or collect names, emails, or accounts to publish. Anyone with a link can view that share, and anyone with its admin key can edit or delete it.

Retention: shares auto-expire and are deleted 30 days after creation (enforced by storage lifecycle rules plus a nightly cleanup job). You can also delete a share at any time using its admin key.

3. The Aster connector for Claude

Aster offers an optional connector (an MCP server at share.asterlabs.app/mcp) that you can add to Claude. It exposes a single tool, publish_to_aster, which publishes a self-contained HTML artifact you provide as an Aster Share page and returns the resulting public and admin links.

  • The connector only ever receives the HTML artifact and optional title that you ask Claude to publish. It does not read your Claude conversations, history, memory, files, or any other context, and it requests no such access.
  • Published artifacts are stored exactly like any other plain Aster Share (see above), with the same 30-day auto-deletion and admin-key deletion.
  • The connector requires no account and no sign-in. It performs no financial actions, serves no ads, and contains no hidden instructions.

The website and feedback form

The marketing site at asterlabs.dev is a static site with no tracking or analytics cookies. Downloads are served from GitHub Releases. If you use the Feedback form, the message and any contact details you include are sent via a third-party email delivery service (EmailJS) so we can read and respond to your feedback; they are used only for that purpose.

What we do not do

  • No user accounts or profiles.
  • No advertising, no behavioral tracking, no third-party analytics in the app.
  • No selling, renting, or sharing of your data with third parties for their own use.
  • No access to your Claude conversations, files, or other connectors.

Data deletion

Recordings and their files live on your Mac. Delete them like any file to remove them. Published shares can be deleted immediately with their admin key, and otherwise auto-delete after 30 days. For any other request, contact us below.

Children

Aster is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes
